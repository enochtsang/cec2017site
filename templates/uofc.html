Founded in 1966, the University of Calgary will celebrate its 50th Anniversary in the 2016/2017 Academic Year. The campus itself occupies over 200 hectares and provides education to over 30,000 enrolled students in undergraduate, graduate and professional degree programs. Famous alumni of the University of Calgary include Stephen Harper, the previous Prime Minister of Canada and Naheed Nenshi, the current Mayor of Calgary.
